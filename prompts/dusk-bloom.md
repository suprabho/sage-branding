## Logo Concepts

**Primary Wordmark:**
"SAGE" set in Archivo at 115% width, weight 700. Generous letterspacing (+3%). Color: Cream Dusk (#F6F2EF) on Deep Plum (#6B4F8C), or Lilac (#A889CC) on Deep Aubergine (#2A1F33). The width gives it presence — expanded, confident, premium.

**Icon Mark:**
An abstract bloom/flower — 5 overlapping petals arranged in a radial pattern, each petal a slightly different shade from the palette (Lilac, Soft Lilac, Dusty Rose). Petals have rounded terminals, slight overlap creates depth. Duotone treatment: front petals at full opacity, back petals at 40%. Works as a premium badge.

**Logo Animation:**
The bloom icon assembles petal by petal — each fades in and rotates slightly into position from the center outward, staggered 80ms apart, with ultra-smooth deceleration (cubic-bezier 0.22, 1, 0.36, 1). Total: ~600ms. The wordmark then fades in with a gentle width expansion from 100% to 115% over 400ms. Feels like a flower opening at dusk — elegant, deliberate.

## Patterns

**Radial Bloom:**
Concentric gradient circles — Lilac center fading to transparent. Place off-center on backgrounds for organic depth. Multiple blooms at varying sizes (200px, 400px, 600px) and opacities (5–15%). Never sharp edges — always feathered.

**Noise Grain:**
Fine monochromatic noise at 3% opacity over solid surfaces. Adds tactile, premium texture. Apply to buttons, card headers, hero sections. Use as a CSS background overlay or PNG texture.

**Flowing Lines:**
Loose, hand-drawn curved lines in Soft Lilac at 6% opacity. Like contour lines on a topographic map or brushstrokes. Use on empty states, onboarding screens, email backgrounds. Organic, never geometric.

**Plum Gradient Mesh:**
Overlapping soft ellipses — Deep Plum, Lilac, and Dusty Rose at varying opacities. Creates a living, breathing background. Use as section backgrounds or behind hero content. Think: Northern Lights in plum.

## Icons

**Style Direction:**
Phosphor Icons, duotone variant. Primary layer: Lilac (#A889CC). Secondary layer: Soft Lilac (#C4ADDE) at 30% opacity. Two-layer depth adds visual richness. Never outline-only — always carry the duotone treatment.

**Custom Icon Set (if extending beyond Phosphor):**
24px grid. Primary shapes filled, secondary/shadow layer offset 2px down-right at 30% opacity. Rounded corners (4px). Organic feel — slight imperfection in curves. Suggested custom icons: Bloom flower, Crescent moon, Heartbeat, Journal, Candlelight.

## Imagery

**Photography Direction:**
Warm, intimate, golden-hour to dusk lighting. Parents in quiet, reflective moments — evenings, bedtime routines, soft lamplight. Emotionally resonant without being sentimental.

- Avoid: bright daylight, clinical settings, group shots, stock perfection
- Favor: single parent with child, warm interiors, soft fabrics, evening light. Intimacy and connection.
- Color grade: warm shadows, slightly lifted blacks, amber/plum color cast. Rich, premium, matte.
- Crop: close-up and medium shots. Soft focus on backgrounds. Subjects often looking away from camera — candid, unposed.

**Illustration Direction (if used):**
Flowing, organic line art. Single continuous line or loose brushstroke aesthetic. Plum and Lilac palette with Amber accents. Subjects: parent-child silhouettes, abstract botanical forms, moon phases, flowing shapes. Feels like a meditation app illustration — calming, premium.

**Image Treatments:**
- Large rounded corners: 24px on all containers
- Soft gradient scrim: linear-gradient from transparent to Deep Plum at image bottom edge
- Inner shadow on image containers: inset 0 2px 8px rgba(42, 31, 51, 0.15)
- Photos sit on Deep Aubergine — no white borders, seamless integration into dark surface
