## Logo Concepts

**Primary Wordmark:**
A wordmark reading "SAGE" in Fraunces serif, weight 600. Letterspacing slightly open (+2%). The "S" has a subtle leaf vein running through its curve — organic, not literal. Color: Sage Moss (#4A7C59) on white. No tagline in the logo itself.

**Icon Mark:**
A single stylized leaf, slightly abstracted — the veins form a gentle conversation-bubble silhouette. Filled Sage Moss (#4A7C59). Rounded corners, feels hand-drawn but precise. Works at 24px and 512px.

**Logo Animation:**
The leaf icon fades in from 80% scale with a gentle spring (cubic-bezier 0.34, 1.2, 0.64, 1) over 600ms. The wordmark letters appear left-to-right with a staggered 40ms delay, each fading up from 8px below. Total duration: ~900ms. Feels like a morning stretch — unhurried, natural.

## Patterns

**Botanical Micro-Pattern:**
Tiny, scattered leaf silhouettes at 4% opacity on Parchment. Leaves are abstracted to 3–4 simple curved strokes. Random rotation. Tile size: 200×200px. Use on section backgrounds and email headers.

**Dot Grid:**
Linen (#E8E0D5) dots at 6% opacity on Parchment, 20px grid spacing. Use sparingly on neutral content sections. Feels like graph paper for journaling.

**Gradient Mesh:**
Soft, organic blobs — radial gradients of Fern (#6FA884) at 10% opacity and Warm Clay (#C97B4B) at 6% opacity. Overlapping ellipses, blurred, on Parchment. Use as decorative layer behind hero content. Never sharp edges.

## Icons

**Style Direction:**
Phosphor Icons, filled variant. Sage Moss (#4A7C59) default color. 2px visual weight at 24px size. Rounded terminals. Never outlined-only — always carry visual weight.

**Custom Icon Set (if extending beyond Phosphor):**
Consistent 24px grid. 2px stroke with rounded caps. Fill on key states (active, selected). Slight organic imperfection — corners are rounded at 3px, not perfectly geometric. Suggested custom icons: Milestone flag, Sleep moon, Feeding bottle, Routine calendar, Growth chart.

## Imagery

**Photography Direction:**
Real, unposed moments of parents and children. Warm natural light (golden hour preferred). Slightly desaturated — not oversaturated Instagram-style. Quiet, in-between moments: a parent reading, a child exploring, hands together.

- Avoid: staged smiles, stock-photo perfection, clinical settings
- Favor: messy kitchens, half-read books, grass stains, genuine expressions
- Color grade: warm, slightly faded, matte finish. Shadows lifted. Never harsh contrast.
- Crop: generous negative space. Off-center subjects. Room to breathe.

**Illustration Direction (if used):**
Bold, reduced forms. Think linocut or woodblock aesthetic — not watercolor, not vector-cute. Earthy palette: Sage Moss, Warm Clay, Golden Hour. Intentional imperfection in lines. Subjects: abstract parent-child silhouettes, botanical elements, simple objects (cups, books, toys). Never cartoonish or infantile.

**Image Treatments:**
- Rounded corners: 16px on all image containers
- Subtle warm overlay: multiply blend of Parchment at 5% over photos
- Shadow beneath images: var(--shadow-card)
- No borders on images — shadow-only framing
